JACOBS WINTER DESIGN SHOWCASE 2019

GroTech @ Berkeley was able to present a working model of the growing chamber (SeedShuttle) to the public at the Jacobs Winter Design Showcase 2019 at the University of California, Berkeley this December. We were very excited to have been able to share and communicate our ideas, models, and projects to others, and were greatly humbled to view other groundbreaking exhibits during the showcase. We were also highly grateful for the opportunity to meet new people and engage in educational and thought-provoking dialogue. This design showcase has been a great success for everyone and we hope to participate in more events involving the public in the future.

CALAPALOOZA SPRING 2020

This January, GroTech @ Berkeley participated in Calapalooza, UC Berkeley's biannual student activities fair open to the campus. Through a day of busy tabling, we were able to reach out to interested students who may be prospective applicants for our organization's spring semester. Because GroTech @ Berkeley is expand the organization through the opening new projects, such as the Open-Source CNC Farming (FarmBot) and the Automated Hydroponics System, new members are needed. Calapalooza was overall a wonderful opportunity for GroTech @ Berkeley's recruitment process, and we are excited to welcome new members to the organization!
