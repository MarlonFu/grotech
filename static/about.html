GroTech @ Berkeley came to its existence by a group of college undergraduates at the University of California, Berkeley. As our slogan “Grow and Innovate” suggests, GroTech @ Berkeley was formed as a result of a shared passion among seven students for sustainable design and agricultural innovation. Founded in August 2019, the organization serves as a platform for research-driven students to engage in innovative competitions, design plant growth chambers, take on innovative agricultural projects, ideate new agricultural methods, and collaborate with like-minded students for a common goal: the future of our food. Our organization has no boundaries, even going beyond Earth to come up with innovative methods for extraterrestrial plant growth.
